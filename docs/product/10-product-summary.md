# Product Summary

*A polished summary of Talvorax suitable for presentations, documentation, and investor discussions.*

---

## What Talvorax Does

Talvorax is an AI-powered career preparation platform that takes a job seeker from application to offer inside a single product. It delivers four connected capabilities:

1. **Resume Analyzer** — scores a resume against a target role on a 0-100 scale with a transparent, evidence-backed breakdown; flags missing critical skills and screening-system risks; then rewrites the resume with one click and exports it as a professionally designed PDF in the user's choice of three templates.

2. **Interview Coach** — runs realistic mock interviews in five modes (by domain, by job description, by the candidate's own resume, by target company, or by revisiting past real interviews). Questions are spoken aloud; the candidate answers by voice on camera under per-question timers, with presence monitoring for realism. Every session produces an overall score, per-question ratings, side-by-side comparisons of the candidate's answers against ideal answers, a topic-level knowledge gaps map, and a downloadable recording.

3. **Minute Talk** — sixty-second speaking drills on randomly generated topics, with live transcription and objective delivery metrics: content, structure, fluency, and confidence scores, words-per-minute pacing, and filler-word tracking, wrapped in score-chasing gamification.

4. **Job Alerts and AI Matching** — user-defined alerts by role, location, skills, and remote preference, with listings refreshed automatically every six hours; plus an AI matching engine that parses the user's uploaded resume and ranks live jobs by fit, attaching an honest shortlist verdict, matched and missing skills, written reasoning, and job-specific resume tweak tips to every recommendation.

A personal dashboard ties it together, tracking scores across sessions, averaging skill ratings, surfacing focus topics, and pointing the user to their next action.

## Who It Helps

- **Fresh graduates** entering the market with no interview experience and unproven resumes.
- **Active job seekers** juggling many applications who need fast, role-specific tailoring.
- **Career switchers** who need to see which skills transfer and which gaps to close.
- **Experienced professionals** preparing for senior roles and specific companies.
- **Anyone improving spoken communication**, from non-native speakers to nervous presenters.

The platform spans technical and non-technical fields — from software engineering, data science, and cybersecurity to marketing, finance, product management, and HR — and calibrates interview difficulty to the user's declared experience level.

## Why It Is Different

- **Full-journey coverage.** Most tools address one slice — a resume checker, a question bank, a job board. Talvorax connects resume quality, interview readiness, spoken delivery, and job discovery in one loop, with each part reinforcing the others.
- **Realism over flashcards.** Interviews are conducted the way real remote interviews happen: spoken questions, spoken answers, a live camera with presence alerts, and time pressure — not multiple-choice quizzes.
- **Honesty as a feature.** Match scores come with quoted evidence; missing hard requirements trigger explicit gatekeeper penalties; keyword stuffing is penalized rather than rewarded; and job recommendations include "Unlikely" verdicts with reasons. Users get the truth they need to improve, not flattery.
- **Feedback that is measurable.** Communication ability is expressed in numbers users can move: words per minute, filler counts, per-skill averages, topic-level gap maps, and score trends across sessions.
- **Guidance built into every result.** Each analysis ends with concrete next steps — skills to add, topics to study, per-job resume tweaks — turning every report into an action plan.

## Primary Value Delivered

Talvorax compresses the distance between a job seeker's current state and their next offer. Users apply with resumes engineered to pass screening, walk into interviews they have already rehearsed under realistic conditions, speak with a fluency they have measured and trained, and spend their effort on the openings where they genuinely stand a chance. The result is a faster, more confident, and more successful job search — with visible, quantified progress at every step.

## Business Model

A freemium model: a free plan for getting started (basic resume analysis, standard speaking practice, standard job alerts, limited interview coaching) and a Pro plan at $19 per month (unlimited resume analysis and tracking, advanced interview coaching across all roles, Auto Apply integration, and priority support). Announced expansions visible in the product today include Auto Apply (automated job applications with tracking), a dedicated Communication Skills module, and a fully customizable interview mode.
