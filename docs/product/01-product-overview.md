# Product Overview

## Product Name

**Talvorax** — an AI-powered career preparation platform.

## Elevator Pitch

Talvorax helps job seekers land their next role faster by combining an AI resume analyzer, realistic mock interviews, timed speaking practice, and AI-driven job matching in a single web platform. Instead of guessing what recruiters and applicant tracking systems want, candidates get instant, specific, and actionable feedback at every stage of the job search.

## Vision

A world where talent and opportunity meet frictionlessly, enabled by smart, unbiased technology.

## Mission

To democratize career growth by providing every job seeker with the tools they need to navigate the modern hiring landscape with confidence — personalized resume feedback, realistic interview practice, and intelligent job discovery.

## The Problem

- Most resumes are filtered out by automated Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) before a human ever reads them, and candidates rarely know why.
- Interview skills are hard to practice realistically. Candidates walk into high-stakes interviews without ever having rehearsed under real conditions — a camera, a timer, and unpredictable questions.
- Spoken communication (pacing, filler words, structure, confidence) is a major factor in interview outcomes, yet candidates receive almost no objective feedback on it.
- Finding relevant job openings and honestly assessing one's chances at each one is slow, manual, and discouraging.

## Why This Product Exists

Talvorax exists to close the gap between "applying" and "getting hired." It gives candidates the same kind of critical evaluation a recruiter, an interviewer, and a speech coach would provide — instantly, privately, and on demand — so they can improve before the real opportunity arrives.

## Target Audience

- Fresh graduates ("freshers") entering the job market for the first time
- Active job seekers preparing applications and interviews
- Working professionals seeking a role change or promotion
- Career switchers moving into a new domain
- Anyone who wants to improve spoken communication and interview confidence

The platform supports a wide range of fields, including software engineering, data science, AI and machine learning, cybersecurity, cloud, DevOps, design, product management, business analysis, marketing, sales, finance, and human resources.

## Value Proposition

One platform that covers the full journey from application to offer:

1. **Get past the filters** — score and optimize a resume against a target role, then export a polished, professionally formatted version.
2. **Practice like it's real** — take voice-based mock interviews with a camera, timed questions, and detailed post-interview analysis.
3. **Sharpen delivery** — practice speaking on a surprise topic for sixty seconds and receive measurable feedback on fluency, pacing, and filler words.
4. **Find the right jobs** — receive job recommendations ranked by how well the candidate's actual resume matches each opening, with an honest shortlist verdict and concrete tips to improve the match.

## Core Benefits

- Instant, specific resume feedback with a measurable match score
- One-click AI resume optimization with ready-to-download, professionally designed templates
- Realistic interview simulation: spoken questions, spoken answers, live camera, and timing pressure
- Side-by-side comparison of the candidate's answers against ideal answers
- Objective speech metrics: words per minute, filler-word counts, structure, and confidence scores
- Personalized job recommendations with transparent reasoning and skill-gap analysis
- Progress tracking across sessions so users can see improvement over time

## Product Goals

- Help candidates measurably improve their resume-to-role match scores
- Reduce interview anxiety by making realistic practice available anytime
- Turn vague feedback ("communicate better") into concrete metrics and actions
- Shorten the time between starting a job search and receiving an offer
- Keep the core experience accessible with a free tier, with advanced capability available on a paid plan
