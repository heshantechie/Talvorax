# Frequently Asked Questions

---

## General

**What is Talvorax?**
Talvorax is an AI-powered career preparation platform. It analyzes and optimizes resumes, runs realistic voice-and-camera mock interviews with detailed feedback, provides timed speaking practice, and matches your resume against live job listings with honest fit assessments.

**Who is Talvorax for?**
Fresh graduates, active job seekers, career switchers, and experienced professionals. Interview practice explicitly adapts to your experience level, from fresher to many years of experience, and the platform covers technical and non-technical fields alike.

**Is Talvorax free?**
Yes, there is a free plan. It includes basic resume analysis (three per month), standard Minute Talk practice, standard job alerts, and limited interview coaching. A Pro plan at $19 per month adds unlimited resume analysis and tracking, advanced interview coaching for all roles, Auto Apply integration, and priority support.

**Do I need an account?**
Yes, for the practice tools. You can browse all informational pages freely, but the dashboard tools — Resume Analyzer, Interview Coach, Minute Talk, and Job Alerts — require a free account with a verified email address.

## Resume Analyzer

**What file types can I upload?**
PDF, Word documents, and plain text files. (For interview practice and job matching, resume uploads are PDF.)

**What does the match score mean?**
It is a 0-100 rating of how well your resume fits the role you described, combining skill match, experience relevance, impact and achievements, project depth, and ATS optimization. The report shows each component's points, the reasoning, and evidence quoted from your resume.

**What is a "gatekeeper penalty"?**
If a hard requirement of the target role is missing from your resume entirely, the report flags it and explains that this caps your score — the same way a real screening process would filter you out.

**Can the system pick my domain for me?**
Yes. Choose "Auto Select" and your domain is detected from the resume content. If it cannot be determined confidently, you are asked to select one manually.

**What happens when I optimize my resume?**
The content is rewritten to better target your desired role, incorporating any skills you selected. You then preview it in your chosen template — Classic, Modern, or Minimal — and download it as a PDF.

**Are my analyses saved?**
Yes, analyses are saved to your account.

## Interview Coach

**What interview modes are available?**
Five today: Domain Based, Job Description Based, Resume Based, Company Specific, and Previous Interview Experience. A sixth, Custom Mode (combining resume, job description, and more), is shown as coming soon.

**How long is an interview?**
You choose: either a duration of 5 or 10 minutes, or a set number of questions from 1 to 10. Each question has its own time allowance based on complexity, up to sixty seconds.

**Do I type my answers?**
No — you speak them. Questions are read aloud, you answer by voice, and your words are transcribed live on screen.

**Why does the interview use my camera?**
To simulate real remote-interview conditions. The session is video recorded (you can download the recording afterward), and the system alerts you if your face is not visible or if more than one person appears on camera.

**What if I don't answer a question?**
You can skip it, and if the timer runs out before you say anything, the question is marked as skipped and the session moves on automatically.

**What feedback do I get?**
An overall score; a per-question rating of Good, Improve, or Needs Work; your transcribed answer side by side with an ideal answer for every question; a knowledge gaps map grouping your weak topics; and key takeaways. Your communication, technical, and problem-solving ratings also feed your dashboard averages.

**Can I review only the questions I flagged?**
Yes. You can bookmark questions during the interview and filter the results view to bookmarked questions only.

**Does it work in any language?**
Interviews are conducted in English. If non-English speech is detected, a warning appears.

## Minute Talk

**What is Minute Talk?**
A sixty-second speaking drill. You get a randomly generated topic — across categories like Technology, Career, Society, and Abstract, with difficulty from Easy to Extreme — and speak on it while a countdown runs and your words are transcribed live.

**Can I change the topic?**
Yes, skip as many times as you like before you start.

**What is measured?**
Content quality, structure, fluency, and confidence (each out of 10), an overall score out of 100, your words-per-minute pace (with an ideal range indicated), your total filler words and most-used filler, and a set of actionable tips.

**Is my progress tracked?**
Yes — your best score, last score, and total number of sessions are displayed and updated after every session.

## Job Alerts and AI Matching

**How do job alerts work?**
Create an alert with a role title, optional location, a remote-only option, skills, and a frequency (instant, daily, or weekly). Job listings are synchronized automatically every six hours, and you can trigger a manual sync anytime. Alerts can be paused, edited, or deleted.

**How does AI resume matching work?**
Upload your resume as a PDF (up to 10 MB). It is parsed into a profile — skills, target roles, seniority, years of experience — and scored against live job listings. Each recommendation shows a match score, a shortlist verdict (Strong, Likely, Possible, or Unlikely), matched and missing skills, written reasoning, and resume tweak tips specific to that job.

**Can I apply from inside Talvorax?**
Each recommendation card has an Apply Now link that opens the original job posting, where you complete the application. You can also save recommendations for later or dismiss them.

**What is the Market Gap Analysis?**
A summary of the skills most frequently missing across all your matched jobs — effectively a personalized list of what to learn next to improve your market fit.

**Can I remove my resume?**
Yes. You can delete your resume profile at any time, which also removes your recommendations. You are asked to confirm before deletion.

## Account and Privacy

**What are the password requirements?**
At least eight characters, including an uppercase letter, a lowercase letter, a number, and a special character.

**Can I change my email or password later?**
Yes, from Edit Profile. Email changes require confirmation from both your old and new addresses. Password changes require you to re-enter your current password first.

**What happens if someone tries to guess my password?**
Repeated failed login attempts trigger a temporary lockout.

**Can I customize how the app looks?**
Yes — four themes are available: Light, Dark, Glassy, and Neon.

**Where can I get help?**
Through the Contact page, which includes a message form and a support email address, with chat support available Monday through Friday, 9am to 5pm EST.
